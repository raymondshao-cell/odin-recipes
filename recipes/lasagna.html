Lasagna Recipe

Description

Recipe of lasagna with classic bolognese sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500ml of red pasta sauce
  • 200g of mince meat
  • 1 tin of bechamel sauce
  • 3 sheets of lasagna pasta

Steps

  1. Step 1: Cook meat on pan.
  2. Step 2: Put 1 layer of meat on baking dish.
  3. Step 3: Put 1 layer of pasta sauce
  4. Step 4: Put 1 layer of bechamel sauce
  5. Step 5: Put 1 lasagna sheet.
  6. Step 6: Repeat steps 2 - 5, 3 times.
  7. Step 7: Put baking dish in the airfryer at 180 degrees celsius for 20 minutes.
