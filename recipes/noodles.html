Cup Noodle Recipe

Description

Recipe for making cup noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup noodle
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 raw egg

Steps

  1. Step 1: Boil water.
  2. Step 2: Open the cup noodles and remove the flavour packets.
  3. Step 3: Break the egg into a bowl and whisk it.
  4. Step 4: Add the flavourings into the egg and whisk the mixture.
  5. Step 5: Add the mixture into the cup noodles.
  6. Step 6: Fill the cup noodle until the water level is the same as the top of the noodle and wait for 2 minutes.
