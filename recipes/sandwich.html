Ham Sandwich Recipe

Description

Recipe of sandwich with cheese, ham, lettuce, tomato and chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of ham
  • 1 handful of chicken
  • 1 slice of American cheese
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 pieces of lettuce
  • 3 slices of white bread

Steps

  1. Step 1: Put 1 piece of bread on table.
  2. Step 2: Put 1 piece of lettuce on bread.
  3. Step 3: Put 1 slice of cheese on lettuce.
  4. Step 4: Put the ham on top of the cheese.
  5. Step 5: Put 1 slice of bread on top of the ham.
  6. Step 6: Put the chicken on top of the bread.
  7. Step 7: Put the tomatoes on top of the chicken.
  8. Step 8: Put the other piece of lettuce on top of the tomatoes.
  9. Step 9: Put the final piece of bread on top of the lettuce.
